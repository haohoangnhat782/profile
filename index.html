Develop a game application for 2 players-Space Invaders

Introduction to Space Invaders

Space Invaders is a very simple game in the style of advanced civilization.

Typical levels consist of a player piloting a laser cannon to battle columns of descending aliens while using shields to block alien fire.

The speed of the alien approach increases as the game progresses, adding to the tension.

A bonus alien spaceship appears from time to time, which offers the player an opportunity to score additional points by blowing it up.

Occasionally, an alien spaceship with gifts appears, giving the player the opportunity to score extra points or restore 3 lives, or also lose points by blowing it up.

The player with the most points wins.
